### 009 - Sneeze Mechanic
**Severity:** high

Bee sneezes when allergy meter is high.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- When meter hits threshold (80), bee sneezes
- Sneeze drops 25% of collected pollen
- Sneeze has brief stagger (bee stops moving for 0.5s)
- Meter resets to 50 after sneeze
- Track sneeze count
